# Executive Summary

## Objective
Assess whether revenue is overly concentrated among a small customer segment and whether delivery performance creates additional revenue risk.

## What was analyzed
- Customer-level revenue contribution
- Order delivery timeliness and delay severity
- Review outcomes as a service-quality proxy
- Combined concentration + risk segmentation

## Key insights (fill with query outputs)
- Top 10 customers contribute **X%** of total revenue.
- Top 20% of customers contribute **Y%** of total revenue.
- **Z%** of orders are delivered late.
- Delayed orders account for **A%** of total revenue.
- **B** high-value customers are currently in a high delivery-risk segment.

## Business impact
A high concentration ratio combined with delay exposure increases downside risk: operational disruptions can disproportionately impact topline performance.

## Recommended actions
1. Prioritize SLA protections for top-revenue customers.
2. Implement early-warning alerts for delays on high-value orders.
3. Reduce concentration risk by growing reliable mid-tier customer cohorts.
4. Track concentration and delivery-risk KPIs monthly.
